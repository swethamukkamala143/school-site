Admissions Process

Happiness and success for children comes from finding the very best school for them: it’s a big responsibility for a parent

We will give you as much information and as much time as you need to come to your decision…school visit? Meet the teachers? Chat with the Principal? Watch a class? Whatever you feel you need, we will do our best to make it happen. We will work with you each step of the way to help you make the right choice for your child. Our admission process is simple and straightforward for all applicants.
Our Admissions Team will call you within 24 hours to learn more about your child, and to discuss how we can help them achieve their ambitions and love every day at school

Students Based in India

If you live locally, we will help you arrange a visit to the school for you and your child. This will include an assessment by one of our teaching staff that is appropriate to your child’s age.
If you choose to apply, we will ask for a completed form, some supporting documents and the application fee. It’s that simple.
We’ll go through your application form and aim to give you our decision within one week

Relocating/International Students

If you can’t visit the school in person, do get in touch. You can submit the application form online and send us documents in the same way. We’ll work with you to make sure you get the same chance to tour the school, meet staff and ask questions…using our virtual discovery meetings! Before your child’s first day at school, we’ll run an orientation program so you can meet your child’s teacher, visit the school in person. We’ll do everything we can to make sure your child settles in quickly and happily.

Documents we need

1) Previous year’s academic record(s).

2) Previous year’s academic record(s).

3) A copy of your child’s birth certificate.

4) 6 passport-sized photos of your child.

5) Your child’s resident visa (if applicable).

6) A passport-sized image of parents/guardians.
